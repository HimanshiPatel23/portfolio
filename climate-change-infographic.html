03 — WHAT'S CHANGING

Four visible effects.

These aren't projections — they're already measured, every year.

Melting ice sheets

Greenland & Antarctica are losing mass at a combined ~400 billion tonnes a year.

Rising oceans

Coastal cities face more frequent tidal flooding as sea levels climb.

Extreme weather

Heatwaves, droughts, and storms are growing more frequent and severe.

Biodiversity loss

Shifting habitats are pushing species toward range collapse faster than they can adapt.
